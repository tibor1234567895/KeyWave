# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Expectations
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Show empathy and kindness.
- Accept constructive feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive conduct.
- Personal attacks or trolling.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers.
